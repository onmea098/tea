How To Make Tea

Tea is a flavourful way of getting enough fluid into your body each day, with studies showing teas can help protect your teeth and heart.

Ingredients

  • Tea Bag
  • Water
  • Milk - Dairy/ Plant based
  • Sweetener - Sugar/ Honey
  • Garnish - Lime Mint/ Lemon

Preparation

  1. Run the tap a little so the water is nicely aerated, and only boil the kettle once to keep the oxygen levels up - oxygen in water helps flavour!
  2. Place a tea bag into your favourite mug
  3. Pour the hot water over the tea bag and stir briefly.
  4. Tea needs time to unlock all it's flavour, so allow the teabag to steep for 3-4 minutes. This is a perfect chance to grab a sneaky cookie or daydream about having tea on holiday instead at the office or home!
  5. Before removing the tea bag, gently squish it with a spoon against the side of the mug - be gentle, too much pressure will make the tea bitter.
  6. Add some milk, sweeteners or garnishes according to your tastes.
  7. Relax and enjoy!
